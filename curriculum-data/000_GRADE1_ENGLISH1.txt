Book Map

Unit 1 Hello!
Lesson 1: Grammar - present simple of Be; New language - act, and, chant, goodbye, hello, hi, I'm, lesson, Mr, Mrs, point to, trace, unit
Lesson 2: Grammar - use a/an with singular nouns; -s with regular plurals; New language - one, two, three, four, a, an, apple, bag, bed, cat, door
Lesson 3: Grammar - use a/an with singular nouns; -s with regular plurals; New language - five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten, cap, desk, egg, frog, gate, pen
Lesson 4: Grammar - present simple of Be; sentences and questions with is and are; New language - answer, ask, fan, flag, name, no, yes, am, are, is, my, you, your, what's
Lesson 5: Grammar - present simple of Be (This is...); New language - English, Sudanese, he, she, hat, hen, this
Lesson 6: Grammar - present simple of Be; questions with Are...?; New language - insect, jam, jug
Lesson 7: Grammar - present simple of Be; question word How...?; New language - black, fine, how, kick, lamp, leg, thanks
Lesson 8: Grammar - preposition in; New language - in

Unit 2 Numbers
Lesson 1: Grammar - in (preposition); singular and plural nouns with numbers; New language - count, crocodile, little, man, neck, Nile, number, the
Lesson 2: Grammar - present simple of Be; question word How (old)...?; New language - eleven, twelve, old, off, on, spell
Lesson 3: Grammar - present simple of Be; question How many...?; New language - how many, mosque, quiet, rabbit, red, there, thirteen, fourteen, fifteen
Lesson 4: Grammar - present simple of Be; There (are)...; New language - sixteen, seventeen, eighteen, nineteen, twenty
Lesson 5: Grammar - demonstrative pronoun this; present simple of Be; New language - lost, monkey, sad, sun, up, van, with
Lesson 6: Grammar - possessive adjective my; question word Where...?; New language - box, house, mum, that, way, wall, where, window
Lesson 7: Grammar - question word What's...?; subject pronoun it; New language - o'clock, time, yellow, zero, zoo
Lesson 8: Grammar - preposition in

Unit 3 Colours
Lesson 1: Grammar - colour adjectives; New language - blue, green, white, head
Lesson 2: Grammar - colour adjectives; New language - bingo, brown, colour
Lesson 3: Grammar - nouns: the (red) light; New language - go, know, light (n), road, rule, stop, they, wait
Lesson 4: Grammar - There are; questions in the present simple; New language - letter, OK
Lesson 5: Grammar - conjunction and; possessive adjective our; New language - our
Lesson 6: Grammar - possessive 's; New language - ball, flower, picture
Lesson 7: Grammar - possessive adjective my; New language - draw, eye, hair, her, his, son
Lesson 8: Grammar - question words: questions with Be; New language - come, please, T-shirt

Unit 4 About Me
Lesson 1: Grammar - have got; New language - have got, lemon, melon
Lesson 2: Grammar - question word Who...?; possessive adjectives his, her; demonstrative adjective that; New language - book, boy, class, girl, who
Lesson 3: Grammar - noun phrases: my ear, a hand; New language - arm, body, feet, hand, mouth, nose, toes
Lesson 4: Grammar - questions with Be; yes/no answers; New language - not, repeat
Lesson 5: Grammar - present simple 3rd person singular and plural of have got; adjectives of size; New language - animal, big, long, match, small, teeth
Lesson 6: Grammar - sentences with have got/-'ve got singular; answers with yes; adjectives of size and colour; New language - fox, grey, hedgehog, hungry, spine
Lesson 7: Grammar - question words Where's...? What's...?; demonstrative pronouns this, that; New language - look at, market, museum, of, photo, pyramid, school
Lesson 8: Grammar - questions with have got; answers with yes/no

Unit 5 My School
Lesson 1: Grammar - definite and indefinite articles; imperative of point to; New language - board, chair, floor, teacher
Lesson 2: Grammar - questions with What's this? and Is it...?; answers with Yes, it is and No, it isn't; New language - car, hard, toy
Lesson 3: Grammar - question words Who? What? Where? with present simple of Be; the preposition next to; New language - next to, pencil, pencil case, ruler
Lesson 4: Grammar - simple prepositions in, on, under, behind, in front of; pronoun them; New language - behind, in front of, under, them
Lesson 5: Grammar - adverb here; New language - bin, classroom, here, library
Lesson 6: Grammar - adverb there; New language - for, tail, word
Lesson 7: Grammar - pronouns this, that; New language - from, Sudan, sultan, table, well, year
Lesson 8: Grammar - prepositions under, behind, in; adverb in
